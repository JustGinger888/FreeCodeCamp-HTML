Adventure (Atari 2600)

First action-adventure video game

A capture of actual adventure gameplay where aspects such as movement, objectives, enemies and interaction are clearly displayed. Interestingly enough this game is one of the first to ever feature the popular "easter egg" trend featured im many popular titles today!
